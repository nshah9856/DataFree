Integrated Technologies

  • Trending Twitter

    Returns the top trending topics on twitter. If not given a search number, automatically returns 5.

  • Trending Reddit

    See top trending posts on Reddit. Links included.

  • Trending News (topic)

    Returns top trending articles based on given topic.

  • Trending Youtube

    Top Trending in the United States videos are returned with title and links.

  • What is the weather in (location)

    Returns current, minimum and maximum temperature in degrees celcius based on given location.

  • Google (search)

    Returns top titles and links on the Google saerch page based on search topic.

  • Gas closest to (address)

    Based on the given location, locations of nearest gas stations are returned and the distance.

  • Directions from (StartAddress) to (Destination)

    Given a starting address and final destination, directions are listed in order.
